Tips, Tricks and Nuts

Quick College Survival Tips

  • Set small, achievable goals – Break big tasks into nuts-sized pieces.
  • Use timers – Try the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes of break (nut snack optional!).
  • Keep a daily checklist – Squirrels love lists (and so will you)!
  • Find your study spot – Quiet tree? Cozy library nook? Discover where you focus best.
  • Don’t forget to snack – Healthy snacks keep your tail bushy and your mind sharp.
  • Ask for help – Even squirrels need a hand sometimes. Reach out to professors, friends, or campus resources.
  • Celebrate small wins – Every acorn counts!

Tricks for Staying Organized

  • Color code your notes and calendar.
  • Use digital tools like Google Calendar or Trello.
  • Set reminders for important due dates.
  • Keep your backpack (or tree trunk) tidy.

“Nuts” of Wisdom

  • Take time for self-care. Rest is just as important as work.
  • Don’t compare your journey to others—every squirrel has their own path.
  • Remember: It’s okay to make mistakes and learn along the way!
