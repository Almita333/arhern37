What is ADHD?

ADHD stands for Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder. It is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects both children and adults. Symptoms include difficulty focusing, hyperactivity, and impulsive behavior. ADHD can impact academic performance and daily life, but with the right strategies and support, students can thrive in college.

  • Difficulty paying attention or staying on task
  • Impulsivity and trouble with self-control
  • Restlessness or excessive activity
