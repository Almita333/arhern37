Who is Pea Nut?

Pea Nut is our playful squirrel mascot and the heart of this guide! Pea Nut is here to help students navigate college life, offering tips, tricks, and a nutty sense of humor to keep things fun and relatable. Whether you’re dealing with ADHD, searching for study hacks, or just looking for a friend, Pea Nut is always around to lend a paw (and maybe share a snack)!

  • Loves nuts, coffee, and campus adventures
  • Advocate for ADHD awareness and support
  • Always ready to help with a big, bushy tail and a smile

Follow Pea Nut’s journey and join the community!
